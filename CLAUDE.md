The purpose of this repo is to gather together a curated list of projects involving multi-agent simulations for exploring diverse perspectives, especially on policy topics.

This includes projects using LLMs and agentic AI for simulated fora, conferences, debates, deliberative processes, and perspective synthesis across viewpoints.

When organising the information into README.md, adhere to these preferences:

- Organise by section
- Use shields.io badges for GitHub repos with star count and last commit
- Short descriptions copied from projects
- Author notes in italics to distinguish from project descriptions
- Separate sections for websites and demos/POCs
